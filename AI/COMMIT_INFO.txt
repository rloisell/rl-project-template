AI Commit Log
==============
Record of significant commits and pushes driven by the AI.
Append one entry per major operation. Do not remove previous entries.

Format per entry:
  Date:    YYYY-MM-DD
  Branch:  <branch-name>
  Hash:    <short-hash>
  Message: <commit subject>
  Push:    succeeded | failed | deferred
  Notes:   <anything notable>
